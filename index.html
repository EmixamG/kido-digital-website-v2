Your new website in 6 steps! (or less)

#1

Discussion(s)

First things first, we schedule a meeting to discuss the structure, design, functionnalities, and the content you want for your future website. That's where you can be creative and ask for the crazy stuffs. Based on that, you will receive a more detailed quote for your website.

#2

Drafts

The structure, the UI/UX deisgn, the fonts and colors, ... You will be able to follow the evolution of the whole creative process while and see your website take form.

#3

Prototype

I present you the final version of your future website. Every detail is reviewed before your approval for the release. Once it is approved, we can setup the backend services to put it online.

#4

Release

Once the final invoice is received, the website is finally put online. You receive three months of free maintenance to ensure that eveything works properly even after the launch.

#5

Training

Time for you to take the commands. For that, I prepare a detailed document to help you manage your website by yourself if you desire. Additionally, once the website is online, you will receive all the necessary access to get the exclusive control of it.

#6

Maintenance

A healthy website needs to be checked and updated regularly to make sure that there are no errors and that all the backups are made correctly. Even after the release, I make sure that everything works smoothly and properly.

Frequenty Asked Questions

How much time does it take?

It depends on many things like the desired number of pages, the level of autonomy you want to manage the website, the functionnalities you want to add, ... Each website is unique and therefore is the time it takes to create it. As an example, a simple website like this one woud take between 1 and 3 weeks to build. A more complex website like an online shop could take between 1 and 3 months.

Can I manage the website by myself once it is online?

I try to build websites in a way that allows its owners to easily manage and modify it by themselves. At delivery, you will receive a document explaining the important things to know regarding modifications, updates, maintenance, etc. A short training session is also scheduled to show you how things are done. If you prefer, I can also take care of the management myself for a monthly fee. This way, you don't have to worry about anything.

How does the payment process work?

First of all, after briefly explaining your project to me, you will receive an estimate of the price. If agreed, a longer discussion to define the scope of our collaboration will occur. After that, you will receive the price quote and an order confirmation to sign. You can pay in euros, dollars or even bitcoins ! Once the prototype is ready for release, you will receive an invoice and once I receive the payment, the website is live and you'll receive all the necessary credentials.

What if my website is down?

I'm always happy to help if there is anything that goes wrong with your website. The first 3 months of maintenance are always free. After that, you can choose between leaving it to me for a fair monthly fee (including regular tasks like updates, backups, etc.), or maintain it by yourself (I am always available if you need help of course). If anything goes wrong, I always keep a secured backup of your website to put it back online as fast as possible.

What are the other fees that I should expect?

Besides the website for which you pay only once, you will need to subscribe to a hosting service provider as well as domain name registration service (don't worry, I take care of it for you). The price may vary but in general, it goes around 50$ per year. You can interrupt your registration anytime you want. I also offer additional services like technical maintenance or content management for which the price is discussed together according to your needs.

Can I combine different services among those that you offer ?

Yes. Actually, most clients ask me to write the content as I build their website. Some need to have multilingual pages, others would like to acquire some traffic through SEO or online ads, ... When we discuss your needs, we also discuss the right combination of services you will need me to provide.
